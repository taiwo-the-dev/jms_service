About Us

JMS Technology Mission is provide our customers with valuable, cost-effective information technology consulting services and support.

JMS Technology Group, LLC is a provider of data communication services, IT consulting & Outsourcing, Network Services and Software Development. We provide excellent Internet and IT support, in addition to project management, strategic and organizational solutions, which can be easily integrated into a client‘s existing operations. We offer advanced technology strategies that are economical, durable and efficient. MBE certified.

JMS Technology Group, LLC is backed by some of the most reputable leading partners in the business, able to satisfy the customer‘s needs well beyond their expectations. Our technology groups specialize in small and mid-sized businesses to provide them with wide range of solutions with high standard network implementations and support services. JMS Technology Group, LLC can work with any kind of budgets to provide solutions that can ensure business continuity for a long term technology success for your business. JMS Technology Group combines education, expertise, and experience that allow us to provide top of the line IT services to our valued customers. We offer a full complement of technology-based products and services tailored to the needs of our customers.

Our expert engineering team specialize in a wide range of networking products, platforms, and protocols with a combined 20 years of experience in the field. They hold a variety of industry certifications including Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), CompTIA A+, CompTIA Network+, Microsoft MCSA 2003, MCP,MCTS, Certified Network Cabling Specialist (C-TECH), Oracle 10g Certified Associate and Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) and more.
